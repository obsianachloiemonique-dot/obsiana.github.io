During this event i learned that its important to be proud of our talents and culture, and that we must be confident to showcase it to every one.

This event is important because it teaches us to be wise and fair when voting for executive positions such as president, not only in our school but also in our country.

Noli Me Tangere

This event is important to everyone because it brings us back to our heritage and it also commemorates the era when the filipinos are under the kastila and how we were tortured by them, claiming our country as their own. This event teaches us to love our country and to be nationalistic.
